## Selected Question

Can SecMI be represented as a system-consumable gray-box evidence row under the
same quality bar as admitted PIA, or is the gap structural enough that SecMI
must remain `research-support-only`?

## Hypothesis

SecMI may be promotable as a supporting gray-box consumer row only if the review
can define:

- an attack row with stable consumer-facing semantics;
- finite-tail denominators and low-FPR language matching admitted PIA;
- adaptive-review comparability or an explicit blocker;
- structured cost/provenance fields compatible with downstream evidence bundles;
- clear NNS auxiliary-head wording that does not overclaim product meaning.

## Falsifier

Keep SecMI as `research-support-only` if any of these remain unresolved:

- NNS cannot be stated as a product-facing risk signal;
- adaptive-review comparability is absent;
- cost/provenance cannot be aligned with admitted rows;
- finite-tail language would be weaker or more misleading than PIA;
- the row would require Platform/Runtime schema changes.
